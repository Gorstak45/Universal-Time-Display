Understanding Universal Flow Time

The Universal Flow Time (UFT) is a streamlined, global time display.

  • Flow Units: Represents a continuously increasing count of time units since the Epoch (e.g., January 1, 2000).
  • Local Day Percentage: A percentage value .xx showing how far through the local day you are, where .00 is local midnight and .99 is the end of the day.

For example, 12345.57 means 12,345 flow units have passed since Epoch, and you are 57% through your local day.
