Understanding Universal Time

The Universal Time is represented as xxxxx.xx, where:

  • xxxxx: Total flow units since the epoch (e.g., January 1, 2000).
  • .xx: A percentage of the current day that has passed based on your local time.

For example, 12345.67 indicates that 12,345 flow units have passed since the epoch, and you are 67% through your local day.
