Understanding Universal Flow Time

The Universal Flow Time (UFT) presents two key time metrics:

  • Days: Total number of days since the epoch (e.g., January 1, 2000).
  • % of Day: The percentage of the current day that has passed based on your local time.

For example, Days: 8760, % of Day: 57.33 indicates that 8,760 days have passed since the epoch, and you are 57.33% through your local day.
